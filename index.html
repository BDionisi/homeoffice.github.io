Home Office
    • WHICH IS:

    It is when an employee performs their company activities at home, that is, doing their work at home. No need to travel to the company, making life easier for your employee and your company.

    BENEFITS:

    Flexibility, Convenience, Quality of Life, Economy, Comfort.

    HOW TO PUT IT INTO PRACTICE:

    1. Use data to choose collaborators. 2. Rely on technologies to measure productivity. 3. Implement platforms for internal communication. 4. Formalize the new working model with collaborators. 5. Take care of data security and privacy.
